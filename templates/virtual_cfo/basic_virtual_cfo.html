What is a Basic Virtual CFO?

A Basic Virtual CFO provides the foundational financial leadership layer that every growing business needs but often lacks — systematic compliance management, monthly financial reporting, cash flow monitoring, and a senior financial perspective available on-call. Unlike a full-time CFO who costs ₹20–60 lakh per year in salary alone, a Virtual CFO delivers similar strategic value at a fraction of the cost, making it accessible for SMEs, startups, and family businesses.

The Basic tier is designed for businesses that have their bookkeeping in order but need someone to own the financial reporting, ensure all compliance deadlines are met across GST, TDS, PF/ESI, MCA, and Income Tax, and provide monthly financial insights to the promoter or management. Your Virtual CFO participates in monthly finance review calls and is reachable via WhatsApp/email/video call for day-to-day queries.

This is the ideal entry point for businesses with ₹0–10 crore annual revenue that are growing but do not yet justify a full-time Finance Manager or CFO.

FAQs

Do I still need my own accountant or bookkeeper?

Yes — the Virtual CFO works alongside your bookkeeper (or our bookkeeping team). The CFO reviews, advises, and manages; the bookkeeper executes day-to-day recording.

How is this different from a regular CA retainer?

A CA retainer typically covers compliance filings. A Virtual CFO covers compliance plus strategic financial oversight — cash flow management, financial performance review, decision support, and advisory.

Can I upgrade to a higher tier as my business grows?

Absolutely — you can upgrade to the Growth or Startup Advisory tier anytime. We ensure a smooth transition.
