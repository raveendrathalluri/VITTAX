What is a Growth Virtual CFO?

The Growth Virtual CFO is designed for businesses in the ₹10–100 crore revenue range that are scaling aggressively — entering new markets, adding product lines, hiring rapidly, or preparing for institutional funding. At this stage, a business needs not just compliance oversight but active financial strategy: profitability improvement, working capital optimisation, vendor and customer financing strategies, cost control, and board/investor reporting.

VITTAX's Growth CFO engages deeply with your management team — attending monthly board meetings, reviewing all major financial decisions, preparing investor-grade MIS, managing relationships with lenders and auditors, and building financial models to support strategic decisions. This tier includes a senior CA with CFO-level experience assigned to your account.

The Growth CFO also builds and mentors your internal finance team — upskilling your Finance Manager, Controller, or Accounts team to work at a higher level, creating processes and controls that scale with the business.

⚠️ At ₹10–100 Cr revenue, compliance alone is not enough — active financial strategy is what separates businesses that scale profitably from those that grow broke.

Who Needs a Growth Virtual CFO?

✔ Pre-Funding Businesses: Businesses with ₹10–100 Cr revenue preparing for PE/VC funding
✔ Rapid Growth Companies: Companies scaling headcount, geographies, or product lines rapidly
✔ Leveraged Companies: Businesses with bank debt that need tight financial discipline for covenant compliance
✔ Capability Gaps: Promoters who feel they've outgrown their current finance team's capability
✔ Exit-Planning Companies: Companies preparing for IPO, acquisition, or strategic sale
