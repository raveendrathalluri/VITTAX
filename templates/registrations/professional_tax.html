What is Professional Tax?

Professional Tax is a state-level tax levied on salaried employees and self-employed individuals by state governments under their respective Professional Tax Acts. Despite the name, it applies to all professions, trades, and employment — not just professionals. PT is NOT applicable in states like Delhi, Rajasthan, Haryana, and UP.

As an employer, you have two PT obligations: (a) Employer Enrolment Certificate (EC) — registration for the business entity, which pays a flat annual employer PT; and (b) Employer Registration Certificate (RC) — for deducting PT from employee salaries based on salary slabs and depositing it with the state government, with monthly/quarterly returns. VITTAX handles both registrations and manages ongoing PT compliance for your entity and employees.

⚠️ PT is NOT applicable in states like Delhi, Rajasthan, Haryana, and UP — VITTAX advises on state-specific applicability before registration.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PT rate for employees?

PT rates vary by state and salary slab. In Maharashtra, the maximum is ₹200/month (₹2,500/year). In Karnataka, it ranges from ₹60–200/month. Employers deduct PT from employees' salaries based on the applicable state's slab rates.

Is PT deductible for income tax purposes?

Yes — PT paid by an employee is allowed as a deduction from gross salary under Section 16(iii) of the Income Tax Act when computing net taxable salary.
