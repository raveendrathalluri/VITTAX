What is ISO Certification?

ISO (International Organisation for Standardisation) certifications are globally recognised standards that demonstrate a business's commitment to quality, safety, efficiency, and continuous improvement. The most commonly sought ISO certifications include ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management System), ISO 27001:2022 (Information Security Management), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management), ISO 22000 (Food Safety), ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety), and ISO/IEC 20000 (IT Service Management).

ISO certification is increasingly required by enterprise customers, government tendering authorities, export markets, and multinational corporations as a pre-qualification condition for vendor empanelment. For Indian businesses exporting to Europe, the USA, or the Middle East, ISO certification (especially ISO 9001) often serves as a market access facilitator. VITTAX provides end-to-end ISO certification advisory — from gap analysis and documentation to connecting you with accredited certification bodies for audit and certificate issuance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does VITTAX issue ISO certificates?

No — ISO certificates are issued only by accredited third-party Certification Bodies (CBs) such as Bureau Veritas, TUV, BVQI, DNV, and others. VITTAX provides implementation advisory and introduces you to the right CB for your budget and business type.

How long is an ISO certificate valid?

ISO certificates are valid for 3 years. During this period, annual surveillance audits are conducted by the CB to verify continued compliance. After 3 years, a full re-certification audit is required.

Is ISO 9001 the same as BIS certification?

No — ISO 9001 is an international quality management system standard. BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification is a mandatory product certification required for specific products sold in India (e.g., electronics, cement, helmets). VITTAX provides advisory for both.
