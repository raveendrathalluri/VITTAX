What is UK Bookkeeping?

UK bookkeeping involves maintaining financial records for UK-registered entities (Ltd companies, LLPs, sole traders) in compliance with UK GAAP (FRS 102/105), HMRC requirements, and Making Tax Digital (MTD) mandates. VITTAX provides offshore bookkeeping and accounting services for UK entities — recording all transactions in Xero, QuickBooks UK, or Sage, performing bank reconciliations, managing VAT, and preparing accounts for submission to Companies House and HMRC.

Our UK practice is staffed by accountants familiar with UK accounting standards, HMRC's MTD for VAT and MTD for Income Tax requirements, and the annual accounts format required by Companies House. We work in synergy with your UK accountant or act as a standalone solution for small UK entities.

⚠️ HMRC's Making Tax Digital (MTD) mandates digital record-keeping and software-based VAT filing — non-compliance attracts HMRC penalties.

FAQs

What is Making Tax Digital (MTD) and do you comply with it?

MTD is HMRC's initiative requiring VAT-registered businesses to keep digital records and file VAT returns using MTD-compatible software. We file VAT returns through Xero/QBO which are fully MTD-compatible.
