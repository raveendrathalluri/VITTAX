What is US Bookkeeping?

US Bookkeeping refers to maintaining financial records for US-incorporated entities (LLCs, C-Corps, S-Corps, partnerships) in accordance with US GAAP, and ensuring records are ready for CPA review, federal/state tax filing (Form 1120, 1065, etc.), and investor reporting. VITTAX provides offshore bookkeeping services for US entities — maintaining books in QuickBooks Online, Xero, Wave or others processing all transactions, reconciling accounts, and delivering monthly financials.

Our US bookkeeping team is trained in US accounting standards, familiar with US chart of accounts, and experienced with e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, and retail businesses with US operations. Given the US-India time zone overlap in the evening hours, we offer near-real-time book-keeping support at significant cost savings vs. hiring a US bookkeeper.

⚠️ Unclean books delay CPA tax filing, cause investor concerns, and can result in missed deductions — professional US bookkeeping prevents all three.
