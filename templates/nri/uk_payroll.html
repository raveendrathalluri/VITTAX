What is UK Payroll?

UK payroll requires operating PAYE (Pay As You Earn) — deducting income tax and National Insurance Contributions (NICs) from employees, reporting each pay run to HMRC via Real Time Information (RTI) using a Full Payment Submission (FPS), and managing employer NICs and pension auto-enrolment obligations under The Pensions Regulator rules. VITTAX runs UK payroll for UK-registered employers using Xero Payroll, Sage Payroll, or BrightPay.

⚠️ Every pay run must be reported to HMRC in real time via RTI FPS — late or missing submissions attract automatic HMRC penalties.

FAQs

What is an EPS and when do you file it?

The Employer Payment Summary (EPS) is submitted when you recover statutory payments (SMP, SSP) or have no employees in a period. We file all required RTI submissions — FPS and EPS — on time.
