What is US Payroll?

US payroll involves calculating employee gross pay, withholding federal income tax (based on W-4), state income tax, FICA taxes (Social Security 6.2% + Medicare 1.45%), and employer-side FUTA/SUTA contributions — then filing quarterly returns (Form 941) and annual W-2s. For small US entities with a handful of employees, payroll mistakes are very common and can lead to IRS penalties.

VITTAX processes US payroll for Indian-owned or Indian-managed US entities — using platforms like Gusto, ADP, Paychex, or Run Payroll. We handle direct deposits, tax withholding deposits, 941/944 filings, W-2 generation, and state payroll filings — ensuring full federal and state compliance.
