What is Government Scheme Advisory?

India's central and state governments offer hundreds of financial incentives for businesses — capital subsidies, interest subventions, export incentives, employment-linked benefits, R&D grants, startup benefits, and sector-specific production-linked incentives (PLI). Most eligible businesses never claim these benefits simply because they don't know they exist or find the application process complex. VITTAX helps businesses identify and claim all government incentives applicable to them.

Our scheme advisory covers: MSME registration (Udyam) and its downstream benefits, PLI scheme applications, state government capital subsidy schemes (varies by state), interest subsidy (CLCSS, TReDS), export promotion schemes (RoDTEP, MEIS successors, SEIS), and startup incentives (DPIIT, state incubation grants, Startup India seed fund). We also handle the application, documentation, and follow-up with government departments.

FAQs

Is Udyam registration mandatory for MSME benefits?

Yes — since July 2020, Udyam registration (replacing Udyog Aadhaar) is mandatory to access MSME-specific schemes, priority sector lending, government tender benefits, and collateral-free loan guarantees. It is free and done online.
