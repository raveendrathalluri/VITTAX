What is Personal Financial Planning?

Personal financial planning is a comprehensive, goal-oriented approach to managing your personal finances — covering income & expense budgeting, investment planning (mutual funds, stocks, real estate, PPF/NPS), insurance adequacy, tax planning, retirement planning, children's education planning, and estate planning. Unlike ad-hoc financial decisions, a financial plan integrates all aspects of your personal finance into a coherent roadmap aligned with your life goals and risk appetite.

VITTAX's personal financial planning service is delivered by qualified CAs and NISM-certified advisors who understand the tax and investment dimensions together — a critical difference from standalone investment advisors who focus only on products. Our advice is fee-based and unbiased — we do not earn commissions on products we recommend, ensuring our interests are fully aligned with yours.

⚠️ Banks and agents earn commissions on products they sell — creating a conflict of interest. VITTAX charges a flat fee and recommends what is best for you, regardless of commissions.

Who Needs Personal Financial Planning?

✔ Peak Earners: Salaried professionals and business owners in the 30–55 age bracket
✔ Windfall Recipients: Individuals with windfall gains (property sale, inheritance, business sale)
✔ NRIs: NRIs with India investments and foreign income to coordinate
✔ Parents: Families planning children's education funding 5–15 years ahead
✔ Pre-Retirees: Pre-retirees (10 years from retirement) reviewing their retirement corpus
