What is a Project Report / CMA Data?

A Project Report is a comprehensive document submitted to banks, NBFCs, and government agencies to establish the viability of a proposed business or project. It includes: business overview, market analysis, project cost and means of finance, machinery/equipment details, revenue projections, and repayment capacity.

CMA (Credit Monitoring Arrangement) Data is the standard financial format required by banks for working capital and term loan assessments — comprising 3 years of audited financials and 2–3 years of projected financials with key ratio analysis.

VITTAX prepares detailed, banker-credible project reports and CMA data for all types of businesses — manufacturing, service, retail, hospitality, healthcare, and technology — using industry-standard templates and realistic, defensible assumptions. Our reports are prepared to maximize approval chances by presenting the business in its most bankable light while remaining factually accurate.

⚠️ Unrealistic projections lead to rejection — VITTAX uses defensible, industry-benchmarked assumptions that banks trust.
