What is Loan & Credit Advisory?

Securing business credit — whether a working capital loan, term loan, overdraft facility, MSME loan or PMEGP loans under government schemes, or letter of credit — requires much more than just submitting an application. Banks and NBFCs scrutinize financial ratios, CIBIL/CRIF scores, DSCR, project viability, collateral, and management track record. Most applications are rejected not due to poor fundamentals but due to poor presentation and preparation.

VITTAX's Loan & Credit Advisory service prepares your business for successful credit applications — from credit health assessment and CIBIL score improvement to CMA data preparation (as required by banks), project report drafting, loan proposal preparation, and lender-matching with the most suitable bank or NBFC for your profile. We also assist with government-backed schemes like CGTMSE, Mudra, Stand-Up India, and ECLGS.

⚠️ Most applications are rejected not due to poor fundamentals but due to poor presentation and preparation — professional loan advisory dramatically improves approval rates.

FAQs

How long does a bank loan take to get approved?

Ranges from 7 days (fintech NBFC) to 45–90 days (PSU bank). Preparation quality significantly reduces turnaround. We have relationships that help expedite processing.

What is CGTMSE and how does it help startups?

The Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro & Small Enterprises provides a guarantee to banks for collateral-free loans up to ₹5 Cr. This means a startup without property to pledge can still get a bank loan. We handle the end-to-end CGTMSE application.
