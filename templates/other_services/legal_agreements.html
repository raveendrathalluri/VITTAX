What is Legal Agreement Drafting?

Every business relationship — with employees, vendors, customers, partners, and investors — should be governed by a well-drafted agreement that clearly defines rights, obligations, payment terms, IP ownership, dispute resolution, and exit mechanisms. Poorly drafted or missing contracts are one of the top causes of costly business disputes in India. VITTAX works with experienced legal professionals to draft, review, and negotiate contracts that protect your interests across all key business relationships.

Our agreement drafting service covers everything from basic NDAs and employment agreements to complex shareholder agreements, franchise agreements, and technology licensing contracts. We combine legal drafting expertise with commercial sense — ensuring contracts are not just legally sound but practically workable.

FAQs

Can you review agreements drafted by the other party?

Yes — contract review (red-lining and negotiation advisory) is available at the same price points as drafting. We highlight risky clauses and recommend protective amendments.

Do your agreements hold up in Indian courts?

Yes — all agreements are drafted under Indian contract law (Indian Contract Act, 1872 and applicable specific statutes). We recommend appropriate governing law, jurisdiction, and dispute resolution mechanism (arbitration vs. civil court).
