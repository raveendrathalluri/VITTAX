What is Accounting Software Setup?

Choosing and correctly implementing accounting software is one of the most important financial infrastructure decisions a business makes. An improperly configured Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks, Oracle, SAP, or Busy implementation leads to incorrect GST returns, wrong financial statements, and wasted hours every month. VITTAX provides implementation, configuration, data migration, and training services for all major accounting platforms used in India and internationally.

Our setup service covers: selecting the right platform for your business size and complexity, configuring GST masters, chart of accounts, inventory (if applicable), payroll integration, bank feed connections, and user access controls — followed by structured training for your finance team to use the software correctly and efficiently.
