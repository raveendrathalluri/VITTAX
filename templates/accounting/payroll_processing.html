What is Payroll Processing?

Payroll processing is the end-to-end computation, disbursement, and compliance reporting cycle for employee salaries. It covers calculating gross pay (CTC), applying statutory deductions (PF, ESI, PT, TDS under Section 392 [old section 192]), processing leave and LOP adjustments, issuing payslips, crediting net salaries to bank accounts, and filing statutory returns.

VITTAX's managed payroll service handles the full cycle for you — from receiving attendance data to crediting salaries and filing monthly PF/ESI challans. Our payroll specialists ensure zero errors in salary computation, 100% compliance with labour laws, and timely filings — eliminating one of the most repetitive and risk-laden back-office tasks.

We work with all company sizes from 5 employees to 5,000+ employees, and integrate with leading HR platforms like greytHR, Keka, Darwinbox, HROne, or your custom system.

⚠️ Payroll errors and late statutory filings attract penalties, employee dissatisfaction, and labour law violations — all avoidable with a managed payroll service.

FAQs

Do you handle full-and-final settlement for resigned employees?

Yes. We calculate FnF including gratuity, leave encashment, and issue the FnF payslip and Form 130 [old Form 16] Part A.

Can you integrate with our HR software?

Yes — we integrate with greytHR, Keka, Darwinbox, Zoho People, and most major HRMS platforms.

What if we have employees in multiple states with different Professional Tax rates?

We handle multi-state PT deductions and filings as part of the Enterprise plan.

Is Form 130 [old Form 16] included?

Yes. Form 130 [old – Form 16] (Part A + Part B) is generated and issued to all employees at year-end in all plans.
