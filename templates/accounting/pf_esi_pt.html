What is PF/ESI/PT Compliance?

PF (Provident Fund), ESI (Employee State Insurance), and PT (Professional Tax) are mandatory statutory contributions every employer must comply with. The Employees' Provident Fund & Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 mandates PF for establishments with 20+ employees; ESI under the ESI Act, 1948 applies to establishments with 10+ employees where salary is below ₹21,000/month; PT is a state-level tax levied on salaried employees in most states.

Non-compliance attracts hefty penalties, interest, prosecution, and attachment of bank accounts by enforcement authorities. VITTAX manages the complete compliance calendar — from monthly challan computation and deposit to annual returns, employee passbook maintenance, and grievance resolution with EPFO/ESIC portals.
