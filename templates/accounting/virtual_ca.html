What are Virtual CA Services?

A Virtual CA is a qualified Chartered Accountant who acts as your dedicated finance and compliance advisor — available on-call via phone, WhatsApp, email, or video — without being physically present in your office. Think of it as having a senior CA on your team for a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire.

VITTAX's Virtual CA service bundles bookkeeping, compliance (GST/TDS/ITR/PF/ESI/PT), MCA Compliances advisory, financial advisory, and on-demand consultation into a single monthly retainer. Your Virtual CA knows your business inside out, proactively flags compliance deadlines, advises on tax-saving strategies, reviews contracts for tax implications, and supports you through audits, notices, and loan applications.

This service is ideal for small businesses, startups, and growing SMEs that need professional CA-level support but are not yet ready to hire a full-time CA or CFO.

⚠️ Businesses without dedicated CA support miss compliance deadlines, overpay taxes, and are unprepared for audits and notices — all avoidable with a Virtual CA.

Who Needs a Virtual CA?

✔ Startups: Startups that cannot afford a full-time CA but need serious financial guidance
✔ Small Businesses: Sole proprietors and small businesses overwhelmed by compliance
✔ Under-Served SMEs: SMEs whose existing CA only shows up at tax time
✔ Growth-Phase Businesses: Businesses navigating rapid growth, fundraising, or expansion
✔ NRIs & Overseas Owners: NRIs or overseas business owners with India operations

FAQs

Who is my Virtual CA — a junior or senior?

Your dedicated Virtual CA is a qualified CA (ICAI member) with a minimum of 5 years' experience. A senior CA supervises all work.

How quickly can I reach my Virtual CA?

Response within 4 business hours on WhatsApp/email. Video calls scheduled within 24 hours.

Can my Virtual CA sign certificates like Form 146 [old: Form 15CB]?

Yes. Our CAs are qualified to sign Form 146 [old- Form 15CB], net-worth certificates, and other attestations, which will be billed separately.

What if I need more advisory hours than my plan includes?

Additional hours are billed as per mutual discussion. You can also upgrade to the next plan anytime.
