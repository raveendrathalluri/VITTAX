What is Cloud Bookkeeping?

Cloud bookkeeping is the practice of recording, classifying, and maintaining all your financial transactions on a secure, internet-based accounting platform — such as Tally Prime (cloud), Zoho Books, QuickBooks Online, Oracle, SAP or similar software. Unlike traditional desktop bookkeeping, your books are accessible 24/7 from any device, always backed up, and updated in real time.

VITTAX assigns a dedicated qualified bookkeeper who connects to your accounting environment, processes your bank feeds, sales invoices, purchase bills, expenses, and journals on an ongoing basis — daily, weekly, or monthly — as your business demands. You get clean, GST-ready, audit-ready books without hiring a full-time accountant.

Whether you are a freelancer, a startup, or a multi-location SME, cloud bookkeeping eliminates errors, reduces tax time stress, and gives management a live view of the business pulse.

⚠️ Traditional desktop bookkeeping creates errors, delays, and data-loss risk — cloud bookkeeping eliminates all three.
