What is GST Registration?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is India's unified indirect tax that replaced VAT, Service Tax, Excise Duty, and other levies. GST Registration allows businesses to legally collect GST, claim Input Tax Credit (ITC), and file GST returns.

GST Registration is mandatory for businesses exceeding ₹40 lakhs (goods) or ₹20 lakhs (services). Businesses below ₹1.5 crore can opt for the Composition Scheme. Small taxpayers up to ₹5 crore can file under QRMP scheme.

⚠️ Operating without GST when required attracts penalties up to 100% of the tax amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST mandatory for my business?

Depends on turnover — mandatory above ₹40L (goods) or ₹20L (services). Voluntary registration is also possible below the threshold.

How long does GST registration take?

Typically 1–5 business days after all documents are submitted and verified by the government officer.

Can I use my home address for GST?

Yes, home address is accepted as a principal place of business for sole proprietors and small businesses.
