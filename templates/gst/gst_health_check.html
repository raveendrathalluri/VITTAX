What is a GST Health Check?

A GST Health Check is a structured, independent review of your entire GST compliance posture — covering return filing status, ITC claims, invoice compliance, reconciliation gaps, liability calculations, and documentation. Think of it as an internal GST audit that identifies vulnerabilities before a government scrutiny or audit does. Businesses that undergo regular health checks are significantly less likely to receive GST notices, demands, or penalties.

The review covers all aspects of GST compliance: your GSTIN status, return filing history, ITC position, rate classification of products/services, E-Way Bill compliance, invoice format, e-invoice compliance, export/LUT status, and inter-company transaction reporting. The output is a detailed health check report with a risk rating, identified gaps, and a corrective action plans.

⚠️ Businesses that undergo regular health checks are significantly less likely to receive GST notices, demands, or penalties.

What's Reviewed in a GST Health Check?

✔ Return filing status — all GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9 and other returns are up to date?
✔ ITC reconciliation — 2B matching, unclaimed credits, blocked credits wrongly claimed
✔ Rate classification — HSN/SAC codes and tax rates correctly applied?
✔ Invoice compliance — mandatory fields, e-invoice, E-Way Bill obligations met?
✔ Reverse charge compliance — all RCM purchases identified and tax paid?
✔ Export compliance — LUT filed, IGST refund/ITC refund claimed correctly?
✔ ISD / cross-charge — group company credit distribution done correctly?
✔ Place of supply — correct determination for services, especially cross-state?

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should a GST Health Check be done?

Annually — ideally before filing GSTR-9. Businesses with high turnover, complex supply chains, or multiple GSTINs benefit from quarterly health checks. VITTAX offers annual and quarterly health check retainer plans.
